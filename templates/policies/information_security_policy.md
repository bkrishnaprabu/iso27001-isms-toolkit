## 1. Purpose

This Information Security Policy establishes the management direction and support for information security in accordance with business requirements and relevant laws and regulations.

[Organisation Name] is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all information assets owned, processed, or managed on behalf of the organisation and its clients.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- All employees, contractors, consultants, and third parties with access to [Organisation Name] information systems
- All information assets regardless of format (digital, physical, verbal)
- All locations where [Organisation Name] information is processed or stored
- All third-party systems that interact with [Organisation Name] data

---

## 3. Policy Statement

[Organisation Name] shall:

1. **Protect information assets** from all internal, external, deliberate, or accidental threats
2. **Maintain business continuity** by minimising the impact of security incidents
3. **Comply with all legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations** related to information security
4. **Continuously improve** the Information Security Management System (ISMS)
5. **Train all staff** in their information security responsibilities
6. **Manage risks** to information assets through a structured risk management process

---

## 4. Information Security Objectives

The following objectives shall be established, measured, and reviewed annually:

| Objective | Metric | Target |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| Reduce security incidents | Number of confirmed incidents | < 5 per year |
| Maintain patch compliance | % systems patched within SLA | ≥ 95% |
| Security awareness training | % staff trained | ≥ 98% |
| Audit findings resolved | % findings closed on time | ≥ 90% |
| Availability of critical systems | System uptime | ≥ 99.5% |

## 6. Key Security Requirements

### 6.1 Access Control
- Access to information shall be granted on a **least privilege** basis
- All access rights shall be reviewed every 6 months
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all remote access and privileged accounts

### 6.2 Asset Management
- All information assets shall be recorded in the asset register
- All assets shall be classified according to the Data Classification Policy (ISMS-POL-004)

### 6.3 Human Resource Security
- All staff shall complete security awareness training upon joining and annually thereafter
- Background screening shall be performed for all new employees

### 6.4 Physical Security
- Access to all restricted areas requires authorisation
- Clean desk and clean screen policy must be observed at all times

### 6.5 Cryptography
- All sensitive data at rest and in transit must be encrypted using approved algorithms (AES-256, TLS 1.2+)

### 6.6 Incident Management
- All information security events must be reported immediately to the IT Manager
- A formal incident response procedure (ISMS-PROC-008) shall be followed for all confirmed incidents

### 6.7 Business Continuity
- Critical systems must have documented recovery procedures with tested RTO/RPO targets
- Business Continuity Plans shall be tested at least annually

### 6.8 Supplier Security
- All third-party suppliers processing [Organisation Name] data must sign a Data Processing Agreement
- Supplier security posture shall be assessed before engagement and annually thereafter

---

## 7. Policy Violations

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract. Serious violations may be referred to law enforcement authorities.
